Strogonoff

Description

Stroganoff sauce is a sour cream gravy made with beef broth that's thickened with flour. It's flavoured with mustard and has mushrooms in it.

Ingredients

  • 600 g / 1.2 lb scotch fillet steak / boneless rib eye
  • 300 g / 10 oz mushrooms
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 150 ml / 2/3 cup sour cream
  • 40 g / 3 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Use your fist (or rolling pin or mallet) to flatten the steaks to about 3/4cm / 1/3" thick. Slice into 5mm / 1/5" strips (cut long ones in half), discarding excess fat.
  2. Turn heat down to medium high. Add butter, melt. Then add onions, cook for 1 minute, then add mushrooms.
  3. Cook mushrooms until golden. Scrape bottom of fry pan to get all the golden bits off (this is flavour!).
  4. Add beef back in (including plate juices). Simmer for 1 minute, then remove from stove immediately. (Note 4)
